Brand Standards

Colors: Navy #1A2744, Orange #E85D26, Teal #0D8C7E, Cream #FAF9F6. No others.
Convention: Teal = yield / good output. Orange = loss / waste in rupees.
Critical numbers: 5,000 kg input → 4,100 kg final pouch output = 82% end-to-end yield. Per-stage: metalliser 96-98%, slitting 96-97%, lamination 97-98%, printing 94-96%, pouching 93-95%. Loss in ₹: 1% gap costs ₹9,500-19,000 per 5,000 kg cycle.

Review Checklist (8-Point)

  1. Brand colours — Navy / Orange / Teal / Cream / white / grey only. Teal = yield/good; orange = loss.
  2. Typography — Inter/Söhne sans-serif; big numbers in Bebas/Anton; formulas in monospace.
  3. Number accuracy (HARD) — Cover: cascade 5000→4850→4656→4516→4290→3990. Flow 2A/2B: WRONG 95.8% vs RIGHT 80.5%, 15.3pp gap. Cheat sheet: 7 stage benchmarks + 4 loss-rupee values (₹19k/₹38k/₹57k/₹76k).
  4. Image format — Images 1, 2A, 2B at 1600×900 widescreen PNG (300-700 KB). Image 3 at 1200×1500 portrait PNG (400-700 KB).
  5. Logo placement — bottom-right (Images 1-2) or top-left (Image 3 cheat sheet), ~80–110 px.
  6. Information density — cover 5 sec, flow diagram 10 sec, cheat sheet dense by design.
  7. No AI-tells — no faces, no distorted hands, no garbled small text.
