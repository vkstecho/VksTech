## 🚨 BOAT SHAPE — CRITICAL REFERENCE FOR ALL IMAGES

**For best results, in your Gemini prompt please add:**
> "Search Google Images for 'TiB2-BN evaporation boat' or 'ceramic evaporation boat metallizer' to get accurate visual reference of the boat shape before generating."

A ceramic evaporation boat is a **FLAT RECTANGULAR BAR** with the following characteristics:
- Long, thin shape (~150-200mm long × 30mm wide × 10mm thick)
- Two flat end TABS at the ends (where copper clamps grip)
- The TOP surface is mostly flat with **raised side lips/rims running along the length** (forming a shallow tray)
- The aluminium pool spreads across the entire flat top surface bounded by the side rims
- Material: TiB2-BN composite ceramic (uniform LIGHT GRAY, slightly translucent ceramic finish)
- Looks like: a flat ceramic ruler with raised side rails — essentially a long shallow rectangular pan or "boat" shape

**DO NOT make the boat:**
- Coffin-shaped or trapezoidal (wide-narrow-wide)
- Deep like a bathtub or scoop
- Metallic or shiny chrome
- A bowl with a small dimple in the middle (this is wrong!)
