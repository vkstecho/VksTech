Brand Standards

Colors: Navy #1A2744, Orange #E85D26, Teal #0D8C7E, Cream #FAF9F6. No others.
Convention: Orange = Micron (μm) side. Teal = GSM (g/m²) side.
Fonts: Inter / Söhne (Latin); Hind / Mukta (Devanagari); Bebas Neue / Anton for big numbers.
Critical numbers: PET 12μm = 16.56 GSM · BOPP 18μm = 16.38 GSM (counter-intuitive: nearly identical area-weight, very different micron) · Densities: PET 1.38, BOPP 0.91, CPP 0.90, LDPE 0.92, HDPE 0.95, Paper 0.80, Nylon 1.14.

Review Checklist (8-Point)

  1. Brand colours — Navy / Orange / Teal / Cream / white / grey only. Orange = Micron; teal = GSM.
  2. Typography — Inter/Söhne sans-serif; big numbers in Bebas/Anton; formulas in monospace.
  3. Number accuracy (HARD) — Cover: PET 12μm→16.56, BOPP 18μm→16.38, both formulas. Flow 2A/2B: PET 12μm × 1.38 = 16.56; BOPP 12μm × 0.91 = 10.92; all 7 densities. Cheat sheet: 12 GSM values + 7 densities.
  4. Image format — Images 1, 2A, 2B at 1600×900 widescreen PNG (300-700 KB). Image 3 at 1200×1500 portrait PNG (400-700 KB).
  5. Logo placement — bottom-right (Images 1-2) or top-left (Image 3 cheat sheet), ~80–110 px.
  6. Information density — cover 5 sec, flow diagram 10 sec, cheat sheet dense by design.
  7. No AI-tells — no faces, no distorted hands, no garbled small text.
