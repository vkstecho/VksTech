Brand Standards

Rules: No identifiable faces, no clipart, no decorative flourishes. WSJ / Bloomberg / HBR editorial style only.

Review Checklist (8-Point)

  1. Brand colours only — Navy / Orange / Teal / Cream / white / grey.
  2. Typography — Inter/Söhne sans-serif; big numbers in Bebas/Anton.
  3. Number accuracy (HARD) — Cover: 225×, OTR <1, 0.04 μm. Timeline 2A/2B: 7 stations with correct dates (3000 BCE, 1810×2, 1933, 1948, 1952, 1965), 1965 emphasized. Barrier 3A/3B: 110/40 bare, <1/<1 metallised, 0/0 foil; "99.5%/0.45%" ratio.
  4. Source attribution — Appert / Durand / ICI / Whinfield & Dickson visible on timeline.
  5. Hero panel parity — Image 3A/3B: three columns identical sizes (middle one emphasized via glow only).
  6. Logo placement — bottom-right, ~80–110 px, white backplate.
  7. Information density — cover 5 sec, timeline 10 sec, barrier 5 sec.
  8. No AI-tells — no faces, no distorted hands, no garbled text.
