Review Checklist (8-Point)

  1. Brand colours only — Navy / Orange / Teal / Cream / white / grey.
  2. Typography — Inter/Söhne sans-serif; big numbers in Bebas/Anton.
  3. Number accuracy (HARD) — Cover: "167 KG"/"1 KG" labels, 167:1/3.3 GT/74 MT in panel. Leverage 2A/2B: 1.5g/250g/167:1/4.5g/125g/120g/27×. India 3A/3B: 74 MT, ₹1.5 Lakh Cr, 190 M, bars 22%/19%/18%/6-8%.
  4. Source attribution — Dhall et al. + J Food Sci Technol (2A/2B). ICAR/NABCONS (3A/3B).
  5. Image format — 1600×900 widescreen PNG, 300-700 KB. NOT square, NOT vertical.
  6. Logo placement — bottom-right, ~80–110 px, white backplate.
  7. Information density — cover 0.5 sec shock, leverage diagram 5 sec, India infographic 3 sec.
  8. No AI-tells — no faces, no distorted hands, no garbled text.
