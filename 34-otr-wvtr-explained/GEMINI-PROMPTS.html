Review Checklist (8-Point)

  1. Brand colours — Navy / Orange / Teal / Cream / white / grey only. OTR consistently orange across all images, WVTR consistently teal.
  2. Typography — Inter/Söhne sans-serif; big numbers in Bebas/Anton.
  3. Number accuracy (HARD) — Cover: Bare PET 110/40, Met PET <1/<1, Foil 0/0. Concept 2A/2B: same 3-tier values + 23°C/0%RH and 38°C/90%RH. Matrix 3A/3B: 10 food products with correct OTR/WVTR. Cheat sheet: 16 substrates correct.
  4. Source attribution — Mocon Ox-Tran / ASTM E96 mentioned where appropriate.
  5. Image format and file size — Images 1, 2A, 2B, 3A, 3B at 1600×900 widescreen PNG (300-700 KB). Image 4 at 1200×1500 portrait PNG (400-700 KB).
  6. Logo placement — bottom-right (Images 1-3) or top-left (Image 4 cheat sheet), ~80–110 px.
  7. Information density — cover 5 sec, concept diagram 10 sec, matrix 5 sec, cheat sheet dense by design.
  8. No AI-tells — no faces, no distorted hands, no garbled small text.
