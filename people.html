The People Behind This Journey

"No one builds anything alone. Behind every line of code, every late-night idea, every doubt overcome — there are people who made it possible."

When you build something — an app, a tool, a website, a career — the world only sees the finished output. They see the apps on this site, the calculators, the blogs. They don't see the people who made them possible.

This page is for those people.

I'm Vivek Kumar. I went to NIT Uttarakhand on a scholarship that meant nothing to me at 18, but everything to my parents. I qualified GATE while my mother prayed every morning at the temple near our house. I built my first apps in stolen hours after long shifts at UFlex, JPFL, and GLS — energy I would not have had without my cousin Ankit, who told me to keep building when nobody else thought it mattered.

So when you see something good on this site, it isn't really mine. It belongs to the three people on this page. They are the reason any of it exists.

🙏

Tell them today. Not next week. Not when you "have time." Today.
