Review Checklist (8-Point)

  1. Brand colours only — Navy / Orange / Teal / Cream / white / grey. No others.
  2. Typography — Inter/Söhne sans-serif; big numbers in Bebas/Anton/Oswald.
  3. Number accuracy (HARD) — Cover: −62%, 4.4×, 3×. Process 2A/2B: Al + O₂ → Al₂O₃ formula on AlOx side; ~30 nm cross-section labels. Decision Matrix 3A/3B: 32.17 vs 12.11 MT/day, ₹15.59 vs ₹46.77 conv, ₹17.50 vs ₹4 EPR, deltas −62% / −4.4× / +3×.
  4. Material name — must say AlOx; never "Alubond" or "AluOx".
  5. Hero panel parity — for 3A/3B both BEST FOR text at SAME point size.
  6. Logo placement — bottom-right, ~80–110 px, white backplate.
  7. Information density — cover reads as "AlOx vs Met PET, three penalties" in 5 sec; matrix reads as "two products, four lenses, clear winners" in 5 sec.
  8. No AI-tells — no faces, no distorted hands, no garbled text, no plastic surfaces.
