Brand Standards

Colors: Navy #1A2744, Orange #E85D26, Teal #0D8C7E, Cream #FAF9F6. No others.
Convention: Teal = safe fit. Orange = borderline / over chuck limit.
Fonts: Inter / Söhne (Latin); Hind / Mukta (Devanagari); Bebas Neue / Anton for big numbers; Menlo/Consolas for formulas.
Critical numbers: Standard met jumbo OD 694 mm · Large met jumbo (50,000m) 887 mm · Chuck limits 600/800/900 mm · Cores 76/152 mm.

Review Checklist (8-Point)

  1. Brand colours — Navy / Orange / Teal / Cream / white / grey only. Teal = safe; orange = borderline.
  2. Typography — Inter/Söhne sans-serif; big numbers in Bebas/Anton; formulas in monospace.
  3. Number accuracy (HARD) — Cover: 694/271/887/51,500. Geometry 2A/2B: both equations rendered correctly. Cheat sheet: 7 OD values (694, 887, 558, 772, 761, 694, 623) and 3 chuck limits (600, 800, 900).
  4. Image format — Images 1, 2A, 2B at 1600×900 widescreen PNG (300-700 KB). Image 3 at 1200×1500 portrait PNG (400-700 KB).
  5. Logo placement — bottom-right (Images 1-2) or top-left (Image 3 cheat sheet), ~80–110 px.
  6. Information density — cover 5 sec, geometry diagram 10 sec, cheat sheet dense by design.
  7. No AI-tells — no faces, no distorted hands, no garbled small text.
